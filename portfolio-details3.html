Details:

A t-shirt launcher is an exciting way to energize the crowd during sporting events, keeping fans engaged during breaks. Many teams use generic launchers, which often have limited customizability, usually restricted to team logos. While effective, these lack the unique spirit that represents individual teams.

A few universities have created custom-built t-shirt launchers to showcase their creativity and team spirit. Recognizing this opportunity, UCF has tasked our team with designing a t-shirt launcher that embodies the engineering principles learned throughout our Mechanical Engineering degree program. This project will demonstrate concepts such as mechanics and dynamics while representing UCF's pride and identity.

Our goal is to develop a t-shirt launcher that effectively engages fans during games and showcases UCF's commitment to engineering excellence and its strong ties to the engineering industry.
